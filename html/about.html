About Me

I'm Maeko, a rising voice in the music industry, I come from the country of Nigeria, I am based in Toronto, Canada. Blending soulful melodies, experimental textures, and raw emotion. For me, music is more than just sound. It's movement, feelings, and reminiscence.

My sound is inspired by the openness of being human, late-night city drives, and old records spinning on calm mornings. I would say my genre is AfroR&BAlternativeIndie, if that's not a genre.. It is now. Every song I write is a conversation with the listener, with the moment, and with myself.

My first song "Not You" is out now. I'm working on my first EP right now apart of this EP comes "Unstable", and "Nkunku". This project is cinematic, and a personal invitation into my world. Thank you for being part of this journey.

- Maeko
